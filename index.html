Who am I? Well...

My name is Noah Peloquin and I hope I get the chance to meet you some day. What I like to do day to day is design and develop websites. I am currently studying both Graphic Design and Computer Science at James Madison University for a dual degree. I'm always excited and ready to get to work on new projects.

More things that I like to do are digital artworks, vector animations, video production. I enjoy working with other creators by helping them with branding, overlays, and custom animations for YouTube channels and Twitch.tv streams.
